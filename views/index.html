Achievement

Over the course of three weeks I taught myself es6, React, Redux, Sequelize, and Socket.io and then constructed this application. Having worked extensively with disabled persons in the past, I was able to conduct research into the best solutions for their needs. This app allows people with disabilities to magage, schedule and contact their attendants and care givers with efficiency and ease. I am especially proud of the infinite calendar, which I designed from scratch and the chat room, which allows a user to communicate with her entire network when the need arises.

Attendant Tracker is a passion project of mine, and it's development is ongoing. I've recently begun construction of a related mobile app and plan to release version 1.0 of Attendant Tracker website and app in the summer of 2017.
